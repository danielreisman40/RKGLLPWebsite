Holly Lewis

Associate

Email: holly.lewis@rkgattorneys.com

Telephone: (202) 695-7763


Ms. Lewis represents businesses on a variety of matters, with a focus on mass tort settlement in state and federal court. She has extensive experience in product liability and multi district litigation, including analysis of inventory and individual claims, drafting settlement agreements, lien resolution, and claims processing administration. Ms. Lewis also has experience making Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests with the FDA and analyzing voluminous FOIA request document productions.


Ms. Lewis’ pro bono practice includes serving as a volunteer Judge for Teen Court in Montgomery County, MD, a diversion program for first time juvenile offenders. She is also a former Wills for Heroes County Coordinator, drafting estate documents for first responders and their families.



Notable Matters


  • Represents Endo Pharmaceuticals, Inc. in the settlement of pelvic mesh product liability actions in multi-district litigation in federal court
  • Counsels company regarding advertisement claims and FOIA requests
  • Assists Special Master Ellen Reisman regarding claims for attorneys’ fees in the genetically-modified Syngenta corn seed mass tort litigation
  • Represented law enforcement unions in collective bargaining negotiations and arbitrations
  • Represented clients at unemployment compensation hearings
