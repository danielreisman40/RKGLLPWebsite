Reisman Karron Greene LLP has played a major role in:

Syngenta GMO Corn Litigation

  • Ellen Reisman was appointed Special Master by multiple federal and state courts to facilitate settlement discussions among the parties in lawsuits by tens of thousands of corn farmers and other plaintiffs arising from the sale by Syngenta within the United States of corn seed containing genetically modified traits approved in the United States, but not approved by certain other countries.
    • The lawsuits included class actions and other federal multidistrict litigation pending in the U.S. District Court for the District of Kansas (MDL 2591), other federal litigation pending in the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Illinois, and several related state proceedings pending in courts in Illinois, Minnesota, Louisiana, Indiana, Iowa, and Ohio.
  • Our work resulted in a nationwide $1.51 billion class action settlement that was reached to resolve this litigation and was approved by the District Court in MDL 2591 on December 7, 2018 . That approval became subsequently became final when all objections were withdrawn.
  • Our Special Master role has been expanded to include both allocation of common benefit attorneys’ fees and expenses and various settlement implementation roles (e.g., adjudicating appeals, liens, communications with the parties and the courts).
    • On December 31, 2018, the Court issued a Memorandum and Order that substantially adopted a Report and Recommendation by the Special Master regarding allocation of common benefit attorneys’ fees.
    • On July 19, 2019, the Court issued a Memorandum and Order that substantially adopted a Report and Recommendation by the Special Master regarding allocation of common benefit expenses.

Pelvic Mesh Multi-District Litigation

  • Since 2013, we have been National Settlement Counsel to AMS/Endo Pharmaceuticals in the pelvic mesh litigation.
    • Negotiation and implementation of settlements of over 71,000 claims in the United States, including drafting of all settlement documentation (settlement agreements, releases) and claims processing (including bankruptcy, probate, and lien resolution), participation in mediations and court-ordered settlement conferences, interface with MDL court and plaintiffs’ leadership on all settlement related issues, assisting client in strategic settlement planning.
    • Negotiation of class action settlement of Canadian claims.
  • Court-appointed Special Masters to facilitate settlement of thousands of claims against C.R. Bard in MDL 2187 and Neomedic in MDL 2511.
    • Oversaw negotiation and implementation of a settlement of the entire Neomedic litigation.
    • Supervised court-ordered settlement conferences in the Bard MDL.
    • Mediations of multiple groups of cases and individual cases in the Bard MDL.
    • Coordination with the MDL court on settlement progress and timing.

Abilify Multi-District Litigation

  • Common Benefit Special Master in In re Abilify (Aripiprazole) Products Liability Litigation , No 3:16-md-2734 (N.D. Fla.), appointed by Chief Judge M. Casey Rogers.
    • Reviewed and approved expense expenditures from the common benefit fund.
    • Tracked hours billed as common benefit time by plaintiffs’ attorneys and coordinated with Common Benefit Committee on assessments and hours/expense monitoring and provided Court with regular reporting regarding same.
    • When a settlement occurred, negotiated allocation of common benefit fees and expenses among plaintiffs’ firms seeking awards from the common benefit fund.
    • Report and Recommendation regarding allocation of fees and expenses submitted to and approved by the Court.

3M Combat Arms Ear Plug Multi-District Litigation

  • Settlement Special Master in In re 3M Combat Arms Earplug Products Liability Litigation , No. 3:19 -md-2885 (N.D. Fla.), appointed by Chief Judge M. Casey Rogers on May 29, 2020, to assist the Court in efficiently coordinating settlement discussions.
